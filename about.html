About the F#.Today

General info

F#.Today is arbitrary chosen list of high quality, strategic for F# ecosysten projects following common principles. It's also (again, arbitrary chosen) "Hall of Fame" for F# developers who are working to make F# ecosystem and Community better every day.

It's not affilated with F# Software Foundation, Microsoft, Microsoft Research or any other official entity connected with F#.
